Lasagne

How to make lasagne:

What you need:

  • 400g minced meat
  • 1 TORO Lasange package
  • assorted vegetables

Steps to make lasagne

  1. Cook the meat in a pan.
  2. Boil the contents of the TORO package
  3. Put the meat in the liquid
  4. Put in a big bowl
  5. Put in oven at 200C for 30 min
  6. Add vegetables and enjoy!
