Pizza

How to make Pizza:

What you need:

  • 1 pizza bottom
  • 200g cheese
  • 4 ts tomatosauce
  • 200g ham pieces

Steps to make Pizza

  1. Cook the meat in a pan.
  2. Put sauce on the bottom
  3. Add cheese on top
  4. Put in oven at 200C for 30 min
  5. And enjoy!
